// about us

More Than Students.
We're Engineers.

Ingenium was founded with a single conviction: that students don't need to wait to make an impact. We tackle real engineering challenges, mentor the next generation, and compete at the highest levels — while still in university.

Our multi-disciplinary team bridges mechanical, electrical, software, and civil engineering to deliver solutions that matter.
